Arte Fotographia

Arte fotographia is the official Online photography competition of engifest conducted by parchhayi, the photography and film making society of DTU.


Themes: To be Announced

Judging criteria:
1. 30% weightage to the relevance to the theme,caption,appeal and clarity.
2. 60% weightage to the exposure, colours/monochrome , post processing, focus, framing, and justification to the subject and overall minute technicalities.
3. 10% to the mass appeal or the number of likes on Facebook.

Rules:
1. There will be 5 themes.
2. The participants can send only one entry per theme, therefore a total of 5 photos maximum.
3. All sorts of cameras are allowed. Excessive post-processing won't be allowed (no panoramas, no hdrs, no filters) please do note that the histogram of the entry sent to us will easily give away the use of any of the unaccepted methods.
4. EXIF to be mailed to us along with the photo. Photos should be in JPEG format with an appropriate caption.

Prize money: Rs 6,000

Date: Results will be announced on 20th February

Entries will be sent to the email ID: parchhayidtu@gmail.com
